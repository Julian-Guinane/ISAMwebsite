Secure, Sustainable, Next‑Generation Navigation

OrbitalNav is redefining the autonomous navigation landscape. We develop advanced optical navigation sensors that provide accurate, secure and sustainable solutions for spacecraft across all domains.

What we do…

Accurate

Advanced navigation solutions using optical‑based, RF‑free sensors.

Secure

Redundant positioning to mitigate jamming and spoofing in contested domains.

Sustainable

Enabling debris detection and preventing failure through redundant PNT capability.

Multi‑Domain

Operating autonomously across terrestrial, LEO, GEO, lunar and deep‑space missions.

Design Values

Novel Software

We develop robust algorithms to maximise performance in dynamic environments.

Customisable Layout

Our modular architecture adapts to diverse mission requirements and platforms.

Small Form Factor

Designed for compatibility with CubeSats as well as medium‑ to large‑class satellites.

Low Cost

Democratising space access for emerging nations, startups and research institutions.

Open Source

Our development ethos prioritises transparency and community collaboration.
